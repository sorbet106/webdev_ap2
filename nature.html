Nature and EcoTourism

Indonesia’s nature and ecotourism showcase vast rainforests, majestic volcanoes, and stunning waterfalls that highlight the country’s rich biodiversity and natural beauty.

Sumatra Rainforest

The Sumatra rainforest is one of Indonesia’s richest natural treasures, known for its vast biodiversity and unique ecosystems. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that protects endangered species like the Sumatran orangutan, tiger, and rhinoceros.

Ijen Volcano (Kawah Ijen)

Ijen Volcano (Kawah Ijen) in East Java, Indonesia, is a 2,769-meter-high active volcano renowned for its stunning turquoise, highly acidic crater lake (pH < 0.5) and the world-renowned "blue fire" phenomenon, a result of ignited sulfur gases

Sekumpul Waterfall

Sekumpul Waterfall, located in Buleleng Regency, North Bali, is a stunning, 80-meter, multi-tiered cascade considered one of Bali's most scenic spots.

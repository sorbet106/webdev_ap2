Educational Tourism

Educational tourism in Indonesia highlights learning experiences that combine conservation, culture, and interactive knowledge. Visitors can explore museums, centers, and parks that provide both entertainment and education.

Turtle Conservation and Education Center (Bali)

A facility dedicated to protecting endangered sea turtles. Visitors can learn about conservation efforts, hatchery programs, and the importance of marine biodiversity.

Museum Nasional Indonesia (Jakarta)

Known as the “Museum Gajah,” it houses extensive collections of artifacts, sculptures, and historical treasures that showcase Indonesia’s rich cultural and historical heritage.

Bali Safari and Marine Park (Bali)

A wildlife park offering educational tours about animal conservation. Visitors can observe exotic species, enjoy cultural performances, and learn about Indonesia’s biodiversity.
